I’m Luzaan, a game developer and artist passionate about creating unique, emotional experiences through design and storytelling.

My work often explores the line between myth and reality, using whimsical mechanics and stylized visuals. I thrive in the magical in-between: between dream and logic, color and code, fantasy and function. I believe games can be art, protest, memory, and play all at once—and I aim to make mine all of the above. Currently studying game development and always learning new ways to blend narrative, gameplay, and style.
